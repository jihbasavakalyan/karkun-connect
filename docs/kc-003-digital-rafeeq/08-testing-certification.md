## Purpose of This Document

This document establishes the **testing and certification framework** for Digital Rafeeq — ensuring the companion is accurate, respectful, Urdu-appropriate, and correctly grounded in Karkun Connect data before pilot and rollout.

---

## Design Posture

Digital Rafeeq certification goes beyond functional testing. It must validate **conversation quality**, **domain correctness**, **conversational tone**, and **architectural compliance** — not merely that messages render.

Every certified message must pass **the Rafeeq Test** defined in [10-conversation-principles.md](./10-conversation-principles.md).
