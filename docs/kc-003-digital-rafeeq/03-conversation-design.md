## Purpose of This Document

This document defines **how Digital Rafeeq interacts** with Rukns — conversation goals, flow patterns, turn-taking, and campaign-aligned dialogue — while distinguishing companion conversation from generic chatbot behavior.

All designs must comply with [10-conversation-principles.md](./10-conversation-principles.md).

---

## Design Posture

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Companion, not chatbot | Purpose-driven dialogue tied to campaign work |
| Urdu-first | Primary language of interaction |
| Respectful | Tone and pacing appropriate to Rukn–Karkun relationship context |
| Architecture-aware | Conversation surfaces existing KC data and workflows, not invented facts |
| Interface-agnostic | Patterns apply across chat, voice, WhatsApp, and future surfaces |

---

## Document Scope

This document will eventually cover:

- Conversation goals by campaign phase
- Dialogue patterns (guidance, confirmation, clarification, handoff)
- Turn structure and user input expectations
- Error, ambiguity, and recovery flows
- Handoff to existing Karkun Connect screens and actions
- Accessibility and low-literacy considerations
- Sample dialogue outlines (not final copy)
