## Purpose of This Document

This lexicon ensures Digital Rafeeq uses **consistent, respectful, Urdu-appropriate terminology** across all conversational surfaces — aligned with Karkun Connect domain language and campaign culture.

Terminology supports [10-conversation-principles.md](./10-conversation-principles.md) — especially *Uses campaign terminology*.

---

## Design Posture

Terms in this lexicon are **authoritative for KC-003 copy and dialogue**. Where Urdu equivalents exist, they are preferred. English may appear as a secondary label where clarity requires it.

---

## Document Scope

This document will eventually cover:

- Core role definitions (Rukn, Karkun, Administrator)
- Connection and assignment terminology
- Campaign and mission vocabulary
- Compliance domain terms (JIH Portal, Ijtema, Baitul Maal, etc.)
- Journey and guidance stage labels
- Communication and scheduling terms
- Preferred Urdu forms and transliteration notes
- Terms to avoid or use with caution
