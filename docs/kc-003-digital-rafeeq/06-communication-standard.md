## Purpose of This Document

This document establishes **communication standards** for Digital Rafeeq — how the companion delivers messages, coordinates with existing communication features in Karkun Connect, and maintains respectful, campaign-appropriate outreach.

All communication must comply with [10-conversation-principles.md](./10-conversation-principles.md).

---

## Design Posture

Digital Rafeeq communication is **purposeful and respectful**. It complements — and does not conflict with — existing Karkun Connect communication flows (e.g. WhatsApp scheduling, in-app messaging patterns).

The communication standard is **channel-agnostic** where principles apply uniformly; channel-specific rules are documented per surface.

---

## Document Scope

This document will eventually cover:

- In-app conversation interface messaging standards
- Relationship to existing KC communication services
- Proactive vs. reactive messaging policy
- Notification and interruptibility guidelines
- Channel boundaries (in-app, voice, WhatsApp, external)
- Consent, opt-out, and quiet hours (if applicable)
- Audit and activity logging expectations
- Alignment with [04-style-guide.md](./04-style-guide.md)
