This blueprint defines **what Digital Rafeeq is**, **who it serves**, and **what outcomes it must support** within the Karkun Connect campaign context. It is the authoritative product reference for all downstream KC-003 documentation.

### What Digital Rafeeq Is

Digital Rafeeq is an **Urdu-first digital companion** that helps Rukns perform campaign work through **respectful, natural conversation**.

### What Digital Rafeeq Is Not

Digital Rafeeq is **not a chatbot**. It is not a generic Q&A widget, novelty assistant, or standalone product outside Karkun Connect.
