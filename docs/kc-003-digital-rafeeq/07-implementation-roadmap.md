## Purpose of This Document

This roadmap sequences **how Digital Rafeeq will be designed, built, tested, and released** — from documentation through pilot — without committing to implementation details in Sprint 0.

---

## Roadmap Principles

- **Documentation before code** — Sprint 0 and 0.1 establish the foundation
- **Reuse Karkun Connect architecture** — no parallel systems
- **Rukn-first, Urdu-first** — primary user and language from day one
- **Interface-agnostic conversation layer** — design for chat, voice, WhatsApp, and future surfaces
- **Incremental delivery** — validate conversation quality before broad rollout
